Privacy Policy

PokerMaid respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information We Collect

  • Player Data: Notes, statistics, seat assignments, and color/avatar customizations you create. Stored locally on your device.
  • Photos: If you choose a camera or library photo for an avatar, the image remains on your device.
  • Purchases: We use Apple’s App Store/StoreKit for trials, subscriptions, and payments. Apple may process data necessary to complete the transaction.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features (notes, stats, avatars, seat management).
  • To save your preferences for future sessions.
  • To validate and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions through Apple.

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

3) Data Storage & Security

  • PokerMaid does not operate servers that store your player data. Data is saved on your device (and, if enabled, may be backed up to Apple iCloud).
  • Transactions are securely handled by Apple.

4) Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: Used for trials, subscriptions, and purchase validation. Your use is subject to Apple’s terms and privacy policies.
  • Photos / Camera: Access occurs only with your permission to select or capture an avatar image.

5) Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete notes, stats, and avatars at any time inside the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID → Subscriptions settings.
  • To remove all local data, delete the app from your device.

6) Children’s Privacy

PokerMaid is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

For questions about this policy or your data, email support@pokermaidapp.com.
